Yoimiya

Yoimiya is a pyro archer and owner of the Naganohara Fireworks. She's a cheerful and energetic individual who is passionate about fireworks and always strives to make them bigger and better. Despite her fun-loving nature, Yoimiya is also a skilled fighter and is always ready to protect her friends and the people of Inazuma.

Yoimiya Details

Yoimiya's name is renowned throughout Hanamizaka. She is the most skilled pyrotechnician in Inazuma and is known as the "Queen of the Summer Festival." Yoimiya, who has inherited the mantle of "Naganohara", is able to give people an unparalleled experience with her ever-changing and spectacular fireworks. In addition to being a craftsperson, she is also the "heroic sister" in the eyes of many children. No matter how big the problem is, Yoimiya always has a solution to save the day. Be it the illusions of fear or the shadows of cold reality, they will all dissipate under the starlight magic of Yoimiya's fireworks.
