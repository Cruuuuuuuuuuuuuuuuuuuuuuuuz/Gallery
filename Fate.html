Genshin Impact Gallery Artifact

Emblem of Severed Fate

The 2-piece set bonus for Emblem of Severed Fate increases Energy Recharge by 20%. This means that characters equipped with two pieces of this set will generate energy faster, allowing them to use their Elemental Burst more frequently. The 4-piece set bonus for Emblem of Severed Fate increases Elemental Burst damage by 25% of the Energy Recharge rate. This means that characters equipped with four pieces of this set will deal more damage with their Elemental Burst, making them more effective in combat.
